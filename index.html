Welcome

A Few Words about Me

My name is Matthieu Kisnorbo and I am a dedicated massage therapist, certified from the Holistic Massage Academy. I am committed to providing personalised care to each client, with a tailored treatment to address specific concerns and promote relaxation and healing.

Stress is the root of various health issues and in our modern society, it is essential to take care of our well-being. My goal is to help in the betterment of everyone who seeks my expertise. I use various massage techniques to manipulate soft tissues in the body, such as muscles, tendons, and ligaments in order to relieve pain, improve blood pressure, reduce stress, enhance relaxation, and promote overall health.

Looking forward to share my passion with you and contribute to your well-being.

Swedish Massage

Reduces pain and muscle tension. A relaxing massage that works deep down into muscle tissue.

Aromatherapy massage

Improved blood circulation. A relaxing body and head massage using our essential oils.

Shiatsu Massage

Improved mobility and flexibility. Shiatsu is a traditional Japanese massage technique that aims to balance the body's vital energy by applying pressure to specific points on the body.

Stress and Anxiety Release

A leg and back massage to relieve stress, anxiety and fatigue.
